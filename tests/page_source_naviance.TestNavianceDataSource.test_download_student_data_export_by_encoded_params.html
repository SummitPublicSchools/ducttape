Naviance & PowerSchool SIS integration available now! NEW

Get the most out of your SIS and CCLR solutions. The Naviance and PowerSchool SIS integration will allow you to have greater operational security, accuracy, and simplicity. By integrating Naviance with your SIS, student data flows seamlessly from the SIS to Naviance, which reduces duplicate data, ensures accuracy, and helps paint a complete picture of the student. Benefits of this integration include: Student data flowing seamlessly from your SIS into Naviance, reducing duplicate data and getting your teams on the same page. End the days of frustration from having redundant and out-of-sync data across multiple systems. Your student data can be synced at 4, 12, and 24-hour intervals, depending on how often you need. You can easily transition between PowerSchool SIS and Naviance to quickly complete priority tasks like entering ILP information, viewing student attendance, or reviewing a Student Readiness Report.
